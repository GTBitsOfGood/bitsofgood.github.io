About Us

Our Mission

Bits of Good is a GT Student Organization that connects nonprofits with talented student developers to advance their social missions.

How we Work

Every semester we launch a number of teams which consist of 4-5 students and a project manager. Each of these teams spends the semester working with a local nonprofit to develop new software with the goal of delivering a functional product at the end of the semester. These teams are mentored by experienced developers from our partner organizations at <GT-WebDev/>, GIT MAD, and GT iOS Club.
