What is Bits of Good?

Bits of Good is a student-run Georgia Tech College of Computing organization that connects talented undergraduate software developers with local Atlanta nonprofits to work on projects.

What can Bits of Good do for you?

Bits of Good projects are mutually beneficial to both nonprofits and students. Nonprofits gain new tools for advancing their social missions while the students refine their skills and expand their portfolios by using computing knowledge to provide real-world solutions.

How can I stay updated?

Check us out on Facebook to see the latest updates about our applications and project progress.

Students

We recruit students of all years and skill levels: Experienced software developers looking for a new project; Beginners who want to ge their hands dirty and learn. This is an unmatched opportunity to enhance your skills while doing valuable service for nonprofits in your community.
