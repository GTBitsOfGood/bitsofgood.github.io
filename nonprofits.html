Nonprofits

What can Bits of Good do for you?

Bits of Good charters projects that are individually tailored to solve a problem the nonprofit is facing. This may range from redesigning static web pages to creating applications that are connected to a database backend depending on what best serves the nonprofit’s needs. Please refer to our “Services We Offer” document for more comprehensive details about what we can build for you. We will work with you to determine a solution that will best address your issue.

Static Web Site

Are you looking to create or update your website? Having an up-to-date website is curical for advertising and to inform people about your organization.

Mobile App

Are you looking to leverage cell phones as part of your work? Reach more people than ever before by developing a mobile app.

Internal Web App & Database

Are you currently using excel sheets to manage all your internal data? This solution is a custom built database and an internal web app to enter & track your data.

External Web App & Database

Are you looking for a way to interact with your volunteers through your website? This soultion is a custom built database and a public facing web app that your volunteers can use.

What is expected from you?

The most important thing from you is communication. This is especially important at the beginning of the process to ensure that your needs are clearly communicated to the project manager when determining the scope of the project. Beyond this, you should expect at least bi-weekly communication with your project manager to discuss progress and address any questions the development team may have. At the end of the semester we will host a showcase event for all the projects and invite you to be there.

How long do projects last?

Nonprofits will work with a project manager to outline a deliverable that can be completed within the scope of the semester. If additional requests are made, the project will be continued into the next semester and a new deliverable will be determined.

How can you get involved?

If this sounds like something you would like to get involved with or if you have any questions, please reach out to us at gtbitsofgood@gmail.com to begin the conversation. We are currently recruiting nonprofits for the Fall 2017 semester.
